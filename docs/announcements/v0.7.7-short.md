# Vault-for-LLM v0.7.7

This patch makes review feedback feel like a real closed loop.

When you accept, reject, or defer a `review-summary` card with
`vault automation review-feedback --write-learning-policy`, Vault now immediately
refreshes the next review-summary card deck and the learning-health dashboard.

That means the 5% human review loop is no longer hidden in the database:

- the decision is recorded as feedback
- `learning_policy.json` is refreshed
- `review-summary-latest.json` and `.md` are refreshed
- `learning-health-latest.json` and `.md` are refreshed
- the CLI prints the next files to open

Safety stays the same: feedback does not promote, archive, delete, or apply the
card's recommended lifecycle action. It only teaches future card ranking.
